Our Story

More Than a Café —
A Way of Life

Green Cucina was born from a simple belief: that food should be honest, beautiful, and good for you. Nestled in Kota's vibrant community, our kitchen blends Italian culinary philosophy with the richness of Rajasthani produce — bringing you meals that nourish the body and delight the senses.

Every ingredient we source is chosen with care. Every dish is plated with love. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a lazy Sunday brunch, Green Cucina is your green corner in the city.
